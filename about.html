About Volt China

Volt China is a premier intelligence platform dedicated to providing exclusive insights, data, and analysis on China's rapidly evolving electric vehicle (EV) market. Our mission is to cut through the noise and deliver actionable intelligence to industry professionals, analysts, investors, and enthusiasts worldwide.

We believe that understanding the Chinese EV landscape is critical to understanding the future of the global automotive industry. Our team provides focused coverage on the most important brands, models, and market trends, ensuring the information you receive is timely, accurate, and relevant.

Founded by Leon

An independent automotive analyst based in Guangzhou, the heart of China's manufacturing hub. With on-the-ground access and a deep understanding of the local market, Leon provides the unique context that goes beyond raw data, helping you make more informed decisions.
